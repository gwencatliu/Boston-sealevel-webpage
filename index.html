Boston's Rising Sea Levels

This map displays the potential coastal and river flooding in Boston under various sea level rise scenarios. The various scenaries, 9-inch rise in the 2030s, 21-inch rise in the 2050s, and 36-inch rise in the 2070s, can be toggled between in the upper right corner. These floodings occur at high tide and in the event of storms with an annual exceedance probability (AEP) of 10 and 1 percent.
